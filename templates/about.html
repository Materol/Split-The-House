Split The House!

As a student moving into second year of university, a new issue arose when it came to housing.

I had to get it myself.

The convenience of a university offering me a place to live was far in the past, and a future of managing bills was the future.

I had found some friends to live with, and realised that as head-tenant, I would need to manage all the money and bills in the future. With my lack of organisation I knew this was going to be tough.

I had to design something easy, convenient, and useful to allow me, and maybe others, to manage my bills across the household.

Split The House was born.

On this website you will be able to create a household, invite members to join it, and send bills to be paid by other tenants. They will receive a notification to do so, and you will see it as an outstanding bill. Once they pay it off, they can remove the outstanding bill.

So what are you waiting for? Join us for a simplified world of managing bills.
